Seperator unit

Separators are designed to exploit differences in physical properties such as density, size, viscosity, and solubility among the constituents of the mixture. This enables the efficient separation of solids from liquids, liquids from gases, immiscible liquids, or gases from one another. Depending on the nature of the mixture and desired outcome, separators may utilize diverse mechanisms such as gravity settling, centrifugal force, filtration, evaporation, or chemical reactions.
