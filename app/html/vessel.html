Vessel unit

A storage vessel serves as a critical component within the infrastructure of a chemical plant, providing a secure and controlled environment for the containment and preservation of a wide range of substances, including chemicals, raw materials, intermediates, and finished products. Its primary function revolves around safely housing these substances, ensuring their integrity, stability, and availability as needed for the plant's operations. Storage vessels come in diverse shapes, sizes, and materials, tailored to accommodate the specific properties and requirements of the substances they hold.
