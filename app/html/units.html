c2 separation unit

A distillation column to separate a saturated liquid mixture of ethane and ethylene. The mixture is contains high concentration of ethylene. The distillate (overhead product) is mostly pure ethylene. For the bottom product, it will be ethane.

Electric room unit

Electric room in a chemical plant is a designated area specifically designed to house electrical equipment and systems necessary for the operation of the plant. Its primary purpose is to safely distribute electrical power throughout the facility to support various processes and equipment

The compressor unit

a compressor is a mechanical device used to increase the pressure of gases or vapors within the plant's processes. Its primary purpose is to compress gases or vapors to a higher pressure, facilitating their transportation or further processing

The vessel unit

a storage vessel is a container used for holding various chemicals, raw materials, intermediates, or finished products. The primary purpose of storage vessels in a chemical plant is to safely contain and store various chemicals

The seperator unit

is a piece of equipment used to separate a mixture of substances into its individual components or phases. The primary purpose of a separator is to isolate one or more components from a mixture

The reactors unit

An equipment operated at specific temperature and pressure is used to convert one component or more into different useful product/s by a chemical reactions.

General safety protocol unit
